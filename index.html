May 2023 Trip

Trip locations: Italy, Paris, London England, Edinburgh

We spent the first week of the trip in italy in Vico Equense, my family and I visited the Vatican, Trevi Fountain, Pompeii and Mount Vesuvius. Then we travelled to paris, when we were there we went to the Louvre, the catacombs, Mont Saint-Michel and the Arc De Triomphe. During our stay in Paris we decided to take a day trip to London England and walk around and see Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, London Bridge, London Eye etc. Then we took an evening train to Edinburgh which took us a long time because a train track broke so we had to get on any train we could going in that direction. On the Way we met an Opera singer that helped us get to our destination, she was very kind and travelled with us. When we finally got there we spent the day at the royale mile seeing both castles on each end of the mile, going to Calton hill and the Scott Monument. Before finishing our trip in Paris.
